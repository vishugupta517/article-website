My name is Clark and living the simple life saved my file

I used to be a pack rat. I had so many things that I thought that I loved, but the truth was I couldn't even apreciate any of it because there was too much.

Things were terrible. I had a job that I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couln't hold a relationship.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I got rid of everything. I litreally put 98% of my possesions in the garage, sold my place and moved into a smaller apartment, and I quit my job.

It wasn't easy at all, but everything around me was in such a bad place, I didn't know what else to do, And it worked.

Now I live the simple life

Now that I'm living a simple life, things are so much better. I'm less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enojoy.

With how much it turned my life around, I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from living a simple life as well, whic is why i started this site!
